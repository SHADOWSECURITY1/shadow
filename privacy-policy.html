Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on this website, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, phone number, and inquiry details. We may also collect basic usage data such as browser type and pages visited to maintain and improve the site. We do not use cookies beyond those strictly necessary for essential website functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiries, assess service fit, and support internal operations. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Shadow uses your information only to serve you directly.

Data Security

All data transmitted through this website is protected by SSL encryption. Access to collected information is strictly limited to authorized Shadow personnel. Data is retained only as long as operationally necessary, then securely disposed of. Our security-grade handling standards apply to your personal data with the same rigor we apply to client protection.

Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on Netlify, which processes form submissions on our behalf. Web fonts are served by Google Fonts. We do not engage any advertising networks, behavioral analytics trackers, or third-party data brokers. Your information does not leave our operational circle.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion, correction, or export of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us directly at hello@shdw.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Shadow's services and this website are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

Shadow may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational or legal changes. Continued use of this website following any posted updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. Material changes will be noted by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact Shadow at hello@shdw.com.
