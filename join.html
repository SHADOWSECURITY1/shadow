Who We're Looking For

The Standard Is
Non-Negotiable.

Shadow is building the most elite on-demand security network in the world. Every operator in this network has been vetted, credentialed, and cleared to operate at the highest level. We do not staff warm bodies. We deploy professionals.

The Shadow platform connects clients with the right operator, in the right city, for the right mission — in hours, not days. When the app launches, enrolled operators will be among the first contacted to take the field.

If you have the background, submit your profile now. We will reach out directly when your market opens.

Minimum Standards
  • U.S. Secret Service, military, or federal law enforcement background preferred
  • Minimum 3 years verifiable protective operations experience
  • Clean background — full vetting required prior to deployment
  • Valid driver's license, passport, and travel availability
  • Professional presentation and client-facing communication skills
  • Ability to operate independently and as part of a coordinated detail
  • Open to both local assignments and national / international deployment
