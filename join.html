Operator Classifications

Find Your Tier.

Every operator in the Shadow network is classified into one of five tiers based on background, experience, and capability. Clients are matched to operators by tier. All tiers are vetted, background-checked, and held to Shadow standards before any deployment.

01
Shadow Elite
The Highest Standard

Reserved for operators with direct experience protecting heads of state, senior government officials, or A-list principals at the highest threat levels. This is the tip of the spear.

  • U.S. Secret Service Presidential Protection Detail, or equivalent
  • Special Operations Forces (Delta, SEAL Team 6, CAG, MARSOC)
  • Federal agency protective detail (CIA, FBI HRT, DUSM)
  • 10+ years verifiable protective operations
  • TS/SCI clearance eligible
  • Hostile environment & international deployment
Deployed OnHeads of state · Ultra-HNW principals · High-threat international ops · Classified details
02
Senior Operator
Federal & SOF Professionals

Seasoned operators with federal law enforcement, military special operations, or extensive private sector executive protection experience. Detail leads and advance specialists.

  • Federal law enforcement (FBI, DEA, ATF, DHS, USMS)
  • Military SOF or senior NCO with EP experience
  • 7+ years protective operations
  • Detail lead and shift supervisor capable
  • Advance work and site survey experience
  • National and international travel available
Deployed OnC-suite EP · Diplomatic details · Major event security leads · Advance operations
03
Field Operator
Law Enforcement & Military

Professional operators with law enforcement or military backgrounds, or equivalent private sector experience. The backbone of most Shadow deployments.

  • State or local law enforcement background
  • Military service with relevant MOS / rate
  • 3–7 years private sector protective operations
  • Surveillance detection & counter-surveillance trained
  • Detail team member, site security capable
  • Regional and national travel available
Deployed OnCorporate EP · Event security · Threat assessment support · Residential security
04
Support Operator
Security Industry Professionals

Trained security professionals with documented field experience in access control, screening operations, and event security. Reliable team members with proven track records.

  • Valid state security license / guard card
  • 1–3 years verified security experience
  • Screening and access control proficient
  • Uniform and plainclothes capable
  • Clean background check required
  • Local and regional assignments
Deployed OnEvent teams · Venue security · Screening operations · Corporate access control
05
Entry Operator
Entry-Level Professionals

Entry-level security professionals with basic credentials, physical presence, and a commitment to performing at a higher standard. Everyone starts somewhere.

  • Valid guard card or state security license required
  • Less than 2 years formal security experience
  • Crowd management and access control capable
  • Reliable, professional, and drug-free
  • Clean background check required
  • Local assignments only
Deployed OnLarge event support · Venue crowd management · Metal detector operations · Perimeter control
