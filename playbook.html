The Book

The Corporate Safety
& Security Playbook.

Written by a former U.S. Secret Service Special Agent, The Corporate Safety & Security Playbook is the definitive guide for organizations ready to build a professional-grade security program from the ground up. This is not a theoretical framework — it is a field-tested, operationally proven playbook drawn from decades of real-world protective operations at the highest levels of government and private sector security.

Whether you are a C-suite executive, a corporate security manager, or an entrepreneur protecting your business for the first time, this book delivers the knowledge, frameworks, and tools to make your organization significantly harder to target.

What You'll Learn

Six Core Topics.

Threat Assessment Fundamentals

How to identify, evaluate, and prioritize threats before they become incidents. The same methodology used to protect U.S. presidents.

Building a Security Culture

How to embed security awareness across your organization — from the C-suite to the front desk — without creating a paranoid environment.

Executive Protection Basics

What every executive needs to know about their own protection, including travel security, residential security, and digital exposure.

Event Security Planning

A step-by-step framework for planning and executing security operations for corporate events of any scale.

Crisis Communication

How to communicate during a security incident to protect your people, your brand, and your operational continuity.

Vendor & Contractor Vetting

The security industry is unregulated. Learn how to identify qualified vendors and avoid operators who will put your organization at risk.

About the Author

The Author.

The author served as a Special Agent with the United States Secret Service, protecting the President of the United States and other senior government officials. Following his federal service, he founded Shadow Protection Group to bring the same standard of protection to the private sector.

His operational experience spans presidential protection, dignitary details, advance operations, and threat assessment across domestic and international environments. The Playbook is his effort to democratize the knowledge that has, until now, existed only inside the walls of the most elite protective operations in the world.
