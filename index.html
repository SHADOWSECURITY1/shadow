Who We Are

The Gold
Standard.

Shadow was built on a simple premise: elite-tier protection should not be reserved for sitting presidents. Founded by a former U.S. Secret Service Special Agent, we bring the same operational precision, threat discipline, and mission mindset that protects the world's most powerful figures — to you.

We are not a security company. We are a protection infrastructure. Every detail is vetted. Every move is rehearsed. Every principal leaves intact.

From intimate executive details to securing arenas of 80,000 people, Shadow adapts seamlessly to the mission at hand. Discreet by design. Decisive by training.
