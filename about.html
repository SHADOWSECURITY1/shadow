Our Origin

Born From the
Secret Service.

Shadow was founded with a single conviction: that the protection methodology developed and refined by the United States Secret Service, the gold standard of protective operations in the world, should not be exclusive to heads of state. The world's most sophisticated security thinking should be available to anyone who needs it.

Our founder, Michael de Geus, a former U.S. Secret Service Special Agent with years of operational experience on the Presidential Detail, built Shadow to answer a gap he witnessed firsthand. The private security industry was large, but the standard was inconsistent, the vetting was inadequate, and the methodology was largely borrowed rather than earned.

Shadow is different by design. Every agent is vetted to federal standards. Every operation is planned using advance methodology. Every engagement is executed with the same seriousness of purpose that the most consequential protective operations in the world demand.

Presidential Protective Detail

Founded by Michael de Geus, a former U.S. Secret Service Special Agent who served on the Presidential Detail, Shadow emerged from the front lines of protection at the highest levels of government.

Shadow was born from experiences few can claim and missions even fewer dare to take on. After years securing world leaders, Michael entered the private sector and saw what others overlooked: a world of people, places, and operations dangerously exposed and constantly at risk.

Sporting Events. Arenas/Venues. Executives. Families. Corporations. The very people and institutions that keep the world moving & entertained lacked access to the same intelligence, precision, and discipline that protect heads of state.

Our Mission

The Mission.

Shadow's mission is to provide the most capable, most discreet, and most professionally executed protective services available in the private sector. We exist to ensure that those who face genuine risk have access to genuine expertise, not the illusion of security, but the real thing.

We believe that great protection is invisible. The best operation is the one where nothing ever happens, because everything was done correctly long before the principal arrived. That is the Shadow standard. That is why our clients trust us with what matters most.
